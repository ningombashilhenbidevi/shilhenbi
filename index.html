NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

NIELIT Imphal, founded in 1989, is a leading technical institute under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India. Situated in Akampat, around 5 km from Imphal city, the institute offers formal courses like Diploma in ECE and CSE, BCA, MCA, and MSc IT, affiliated with Manipur University. It also provides non-formal courses such as NIELIT ‘O’ Level and short-term certifications in emerging areas like AI, Big Data, and Office Automation. With modern facilities and labs, NIELIT Imphal promotes digital literacy, skill development, and offers scholarships to SC/ST/PH/female students, significantly contributing to the region’s technological growth.

The NIELIT Imphal campus is well-equipped with modern infrastructure to support quality education and training. Facilities include smart classrooms, computer labs with high-speed internet, electronics and hardware labs, a digital library, seminar halls, and mechanical workshops. The campus also has hostel accommodations for students, a cafeteria, and recreational areas, providing a comfortable and holistic learning environment. Admission to formal courses like Diploma, BCA, MCA, and MSc IT is conducted based on eligibility criteria set by Manipur University and through entrance tests or academic merit, depending on the program. For non-formal and short-term courses, candidates can apply directly to the institute, with admissions usually on a first-come, first-served basis or through scheduled batches. Scholarships and fee waivers are available for eligible students, especially from underprivileged backgrounds. The institute offers a wide range of courses under both formal and non-formal education. Formal programs include: Diploma in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), Diploma in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA), Master of Computer Applications (MCA), M.Sc. in Information Technology (IT). Non-formal courses include: NIELIT 'O' Level in IT and Multimedia, Certificate courses in Artificial Intelligence, Big Data Analytics, Cyber Security, Cloud Computing, Office Automation, and Hardware Maintenance, Specialized programs in Software Development, Web Designing, and Mobile App Development. These courses are designed to equip students with the knowledge and practical skills required in today’s technology-driven job market. With its industry-aligned curriculum, strong academic support, and government affiliation, NIELIT Imphal continues to be a vital institution for IT and electronics education in the Northeast region.
